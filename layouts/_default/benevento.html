We're so excited you'll be joining us for our wedding celebration. In addition to the festivities, we hope you'll take some time to explore all that Benevento and the surrounding area have to offer!

Things to See in Benevento

Arco di Traiano - This magnificent Roman triumphal arch was built in 114 AD and is one of the best-preserved examples of its kind.
Church of Santa Sofia - A UNESCO World Heritage Site, this stunning church dates back to the 8th century and boasts a unique star-shaped plan and remarkable frescoes.
Roman Theatre - Explore the ruins of this ancient Roman theater, a testament to Benevento's rich history.
Duomo - A beautiful cathedral with a fascinating history, with parts of the structure dating back to the 13th century.
Take a Stroll and Enjoy the Food - Benevento has charming streets with nice restaurants and cafes. Be sure to try some of the local specialties!

Day Trip Adventures from Benevento

Napoli - Just over an hour away, Naples is a vibrant and bustling city packed with history, art, and incredible pizza! Explore the historic center, see world-class museums, and walk along the scenic waterfront.
Pompei - Step back in time with a visit to the remarkably preserved ruins of Pompeii, an ancient Roman city buried by the eruption of Mount Vesuvius in 79 AD.
Capri - Escape to the idyllic island of Capri with its Blue Grotta, luxurious villas, and breathtaking coastal views.
Amalfi Coast - Explore the beauty of the Amalfi Coast, with its dramatic cliffs, quaint towns like Positano and Amalfi, and stunning coastal scenery.
